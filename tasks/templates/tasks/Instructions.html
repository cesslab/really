This part of the experiment consists of two questions. One of the two questions will be chosen at random for payment. Once everyone has made their choices we will toss a coin to decide the question we will pay for. If Heads comes up question 1 will be chosen, if Tails comes up it will be question 2.

Tokens earned from either of the selected questions will be converted into US dollars using the rate 1 token = 1 cent and added to you payment in the main part of the experiment.
